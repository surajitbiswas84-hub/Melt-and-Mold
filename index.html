Our Journey

THE BRASS WORLD was born in 2024 out of a shared passion and a life-changing journey. We didn't start in a boardroom; our story began in the rural heartlands of West Bengal. My partner and I traveled to the villages of the Dhokra Damar tribes, a traditional blacksmith community that has kept the magic of metal casting alive for generations.

We didn't just visit; we lived alongside the artisans. We shared meals in their homes, listened to their stories, and spent hours closely observing the mesmerizing, meticulous "lost-wax" casting process. We saw firsthand the immense skill, patience, and soul poured into every single piece.

The Dhokra Damar people have historically embraced a semi-nomadic lifestyle, spreading their unique metallurgical skills across the tribal areas of West Bengal, Jharkhand, Orissa, Chhattisgarh, and Andhra Pradesh. They are the true guardians of India's ancient heritage.

Leaving the village, we knew we had to do something to help preserve this incredible legacy. THE BRASS WORLD was created to bridge the gap between these master artisans and the rest of the world. By bringing these authentic, rustic creations directly to you, we aim to support the tribal communities, provide them with a sustainable livelihood, and ensure that the art of Dokra not only survives but thrives.
