Our Journey

THE BRASS WORLD was founded in 2024 by a vision to preserve the soulful heritage of the Dhokra Damar tribes. We didn't just source these products; we lived in their villages, shared their meals, and closely observed the meticulous 4,000-year-old "lost-wax" casting process. Every piece you see is a testament to an ancient blacksmith community spanning West Bengal, Odisha, and beyond. By choosing us, you are keeping this flame alive and supporting sustainable tribal livelihoods.
