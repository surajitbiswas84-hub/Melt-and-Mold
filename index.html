Our Journey

THE BRASS WORLD was born in 2024. We traveled to the villages of the Dhokra Damar tribes in West Bengal, lived with the artisans, and shared their meals. We saw the meticulous lost-wax process and knew we had to help preserve this 4,000-year-old art form. Every piece you wear supports these tribal families and keeps history alive.
